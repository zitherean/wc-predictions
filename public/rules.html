Scoring

  • Exact score: 5 points.
  • Correct result: 3 points for the right winner or draw.
  • Knockout bonus: +2 points for correctly guessing the team that advances.
  • Wrong result: 0 points.

Knockout matches still use the 5-3-0 scoring base, but a correct advance prediction earns an extra 2 points even when the match is decided by penalties.

Prediction deadlines

You can update predictions until kickoff time. Once the match has started, the score fields are locked.

Match status

  • Upcoming: Predictions are open.
  • Live: Match is in progress.
  • Finished: Results are final.

Fair play

This is a friendly game with no real money involved. Predict honestly and enjoy the competition.
